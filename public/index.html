Our Story

Building the future without forgetting our roots.

Long before mission statements, Burundian elders greeted one another with words that carried far more than "hello." Every greeting was a small transmission — of identity, of blessing, of belonging to something larger than the moment.

Giraso is named for that tradition. We're a technology company inspired by the values our grandparents wove into everyday conversation, building software, platforms and digital infrastructure for Africa and the world — with the same care those words once carried.

We don't see heritage and innovation as opposites. One gives the other direction. It's how we design products: precise, modern, and unmistakably ours.
